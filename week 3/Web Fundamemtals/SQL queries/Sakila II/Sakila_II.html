Get all the list of customers as well as their address, city name, and the country.

9 / 13
Get all the list of customers who live in a country where the country name's length is less than 5. Show the customer with the longest full name first. (Hint: Look into how you can concatenate a string in SQL).
SELECT CONCAT(first_name," ",last_name) AS customer_name, country, CHAR_LENGTH(country) AS country_name_length FROM customer LEFT JOIN address ON customer.address_id = address.address_id LEFT JOIN city ON address.city_id = city.city_id LEFT JOIN country ON city.country_id = country.country_id WHERE CHAR_LENGTH(country) < 5 ORDER BY CHAR_LENGTH(country) DESC;
10 / 13
Get all the list of customers who live in a city name whose length is more than 10. Order the result so that the customers who live in the same country are shown together.
SELECT CONCAT(first_name," ",last_name) AS customer_name, country, city, CHAR_LENGTH(city) AS city_name_length FROM customer LEFT JOIN address ON customer.address_id = address.address_id LEFT JOIN city ON address.city_id = city.city_id LEFT JOIN country ON city.country_id = country.country_id WHERE CHAR_LENGTH(city) > 10 ORDER BY country, city ASC;
